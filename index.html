KPlot.py


WebDev


At the moment KPlot provides a flexible csv reader and a non-bitmap vector text feature.
Also included is a matlabUtility for quickly exporting data from python to matLab.

Future Plans


I intend to include several plotting features In addition to several clustering and dimensional reduction methods. Additionally there are several output formats for plots beyond 2d images. Libraries such as three.js could allow for good browsable high dimensional content. The idea of generating "notebooks" as we do our analysis is a good one.)
